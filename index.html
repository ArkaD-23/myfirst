Scenario of India's qualification to FIFA World Cup 2026

Pot Scenario :

India are currently the last-placed team in Pot 2. If they can hold on to this, they will have to face only one of the Pot 1 teams - Japan, Iran, Australia, South Korea, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Iraq, United Arab Emirates and Oman - in the second round.

All Pot 2 teams - Uzbekistan, China, Jordan, Bahrain, Syria, Vietnam, Palestine, Kyrgyzstan - would be successfully avoided.

Criteria for qualification to Third Round :

The first and second placed team in a group proceeds to the next Round and automatically qualifies for AFC Asia Cup.
India is expected to easily qualify for third round with resistance coming mainly from the third pot team , though recent form has shown that India can defeat a team lower than it's rank.
In the past, India has shown resistance to pot 1 teams like Qatar and Oman. Indian fans therefore can be optimistic of getting atleast a draw if not a win with a pot 1 team.
